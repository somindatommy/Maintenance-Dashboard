And finally, keep special attention to the following.

  1. Don’t stay blocked due to any technical/non-technical issues for more than a few hours. Escalate to the patch lead.
  2. Keep an eye on the provided ETAs and escalate to the patch lead if you feel the fix requires more time. It's ok to revise ETA in an unavoidable situation. But it's NOT ok to do it in the last minute or after the ETA has passed.
  3. Update the Maintenance Dashboard with each state change (Inprogress, PR Sent, Pr Merged etc.). Don’t wait to do it later, as we record timestamps of the changes to be analyzed later.

Let’s follow the process correctly and produce more reliable and efficient fixes.

Happy Patching!
